Private & Small Group Tours to Scandinavia

Take a ride on the world's most scenic railway, have an impromptu photo session while sailing past magnificent fjords, savor a wide range of locally brewed beers, and enjoy evening walks along the cozy streets of tranquil Scandinavian towns. Your tour of Scandinavia promises a lifetime of memories! The custom-designed Scandinavia itineraries below are examples of programs which are put together by Firebird Tours' travel experts.

Tours

  • Sweden Ice Hotel

    Sweden’s Ice Hotel is located north of the Arctic Circle in Sweden Lapland. It was the first Ice Hotel to be constructed anywhere in the world!

  • Fjord & Stockholm

    Experience Stockholm - a city built on 14 islands. Continue to the capital of Norway, before the journey goes to the spectacular Norwegian fjords and Bergen.

  • Swedish Panorama

    Visit the Swedish capital , travel to the heart of Sweden and discover Dalarna with its green hills and forests dotted with red wooden houses.

  • Above the Arctic Circle

    With Rovaniemi as your starting point you will travel Above the Arctic Circle in the Land of the Midnight Sun. Travel across Norway's Lofoten Islands.

  • Nordic Capitals & Fjords

    Combine Bergen, the Gateway to the Fjords, with the Oslo, Copenhagen, Stockholm and Helsinki. Travel on scenic railways and enjoy a cruise on the Nærøyfjord.

  • Göta Canal: The Cruise

    Experience the unique atmosphere on board the historical ship MS Juno on this classical 4-day Göta Canal cruise from Gothenburg to Stockholm.

  • TreeHouse & Icebreaker

    Visit Helsinki and travel by overnight train to Rovaniemi. Stay three nights at the Arctic TreeHouse Hotel and embark on a 3-hour icebreaker cruise on the Bothnian Sea.

  • Volcanoes & Glaciers

    Iceland is a paradise for nature lovers with its pure and unpolluted landscapes. This tour will show you the best natural glimpses of southern Iceland.
